Careers Site Is Easier Than Ever

It's very easy to start using Hireal. Sign up, start creating your job openings and career site is prepared for you automatically.

  • Use one of our templates or customize manually.
  • Import jobs from your favourite recruiting software such as Greenhouse.
  • Embed the job board into your page or have it standalone.

Job Openings Should Pop

Job openings should not be a wall of text and vague descriptions. We believe a job opening should not be any less catchy than your landing pages whily providing information on point.

  • Rigid and clear job descriptions without fluff to save everyones time.
  • Tips on how to write a perfect job description based on latest survey data.
  • Put the target team in the front row, let candidates know how proud of them you are.

Let Job Openings Talk

A dedicated FAQ section tailored to each job opening answers common questions by employees themselves. Do not play broken telephone, skip Candidate - Recruiter - Employee chain and allow candidate to get exact information first hand.

  • Should a candidate has a question not covered in FAQ, it can be posted directly to the team.
  • Employees from the target team get discrete emails to collect answers.
  • Moderation process supported by your Recruiting team and/or a hiring manager.
